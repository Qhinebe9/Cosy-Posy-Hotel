Discover Our Services

Conference and Events

Our Conference Centre boasts a 1500 capacity events hall, boardroom, coffee shop and 2 Meeting and functions that can accommodate 20 and 60 pax respectively.

Dining

Our dining room provides a cosy setting to enjoy a tasty meal. Open daily for breakfast and dinner, the restaurant serves a delicious breakfast buffet and carvery dinner, as well as a traditional Sunday lunch carvery on selected days.

Gym

Our in-house gym conveniently enables you to maintain your fitness routine whilst you’re away.

Spa

Our spa services offer a well-deserved time out for our guests, designed to provide relaxation, rest and revitalisation of the soul, giving our guests a refreshed perspective and renewed energy.
