Welcome To Cosy Posy.

Welcome to Cosy Posy Boutique Hotel, your home away from home. Established in 2006, we are a 4-star graded, 26-room establishment dedicated to offering a unique blend of comfort, elegance, and personalized service.
Proudly 100% female-owned, our hotel reflects a passion for hospitality, attention to detail, and a commitment to creating memorable stays for our guests. Whether you're traveling for business or leisure, we strive to provide an inviting and tranquil retreat that feels just right.
